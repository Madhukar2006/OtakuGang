Server Laws

Strict No-PvP Policy

Combat between players is strictly prohibited. We are a peaceful community focused on building and progression.

Ownership Respect

Do not take items from others without explicit permission. Always ask before borrowing or trading resources.

Active Voice Comm

Keep your microphone active while playing. Communication is key to maintaining our gang's coordination.

Mutual Support

Always extend a helping hand to fellow gang members. If someone is struggling with a build or resources, help them out.

Zero Griefing Tolerance

Destroying or altering another player's structure will result in an immediate and permanent ban from the server.

Anti-Cheat Protocol

The use of X-Ray, fly-hacks, or any 3rd party advantage is forbidden. Play fair and keep the economy balanced.
